About Image Optimization

This tool optimizes images by re-encoding them as JPEGs at your chosen quality level. Lower quality means smaller file sizes.

PNG, GIF, and WebP images with transparency will have their transparent backgrounds converted to white when output as JPEG.

Results depend on the original image and the selected quality. Experiment to find the best balance for your needs!
